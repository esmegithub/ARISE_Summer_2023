# Clusters
In order to decide where to put FloodNet FloodSensors we need to figure out which areas are expirencing the worst floods, and or are at risk for expirencing the worst floods. Although one may think to just look at where the most water flooded, the situation is more complex. There are areas in NYC that expirence more floodwater, but are less populous than areas which expirence almost as much floodwater, but are far more densely populated. In the table below, we took gathered data about which areas expirence the worst flooding, by amount of floodwater and by population and divided each of the highest risk areas into _clusters_ which allow us to look at which general areas in NYC may benefit from a FloodNet FloodSensor.
